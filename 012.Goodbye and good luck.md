# Lesson 12 Goodbye and good luck

## 单词讲解

1. luck n. 运气，幸运

   Good luck! 祝福别人用

   Bad luck! 倒霉，形容自己可用

   lucky adj. 幸运的

   luckily adv. 幸运地



2. sail v. 航行

   sail across the Atlantic 横渡大西洋

   across prep. 横穿

   sailor n. 海员，水手

   sailing n. 航行，航海

   go sailing 去航海



3. harbour n. 港口

   at the harbour 在港口

   the Pearl Harbour 珍珠港



4. proud adj. 自豪

   be proud of ...  以...自豪；介词后接名词、代词、动名词，构成介宾结构

   I am proud of you. 我为你感到骄傲

   We are very proud of him. 我们为他而感到骄傲

   

5. important adj. 重要的

   以t结尾的形容词要变成名词时，变成对应的ce

   importance n. 重要，重要性

   significant adj. 显著的；有重大意义的

   vital adj. 必不可少的；对...极重要的；生死攸关的；

   essential adj. 本质的；精华的；核心的



## 课文语法讲解

### 本课重点

1. 一般将来时
2. 非延续性动词 + 一段时间
3. be 动词搭配的词组



### 一般将来时

参考：一般将来时.md





### 课文讲解

1. Our neighbour, Caption Charles Alison, **will sail** from Portsmouth tomorrow.

   Caption Charles Alison 同位语，解释前面的 neighbour

   tomorrow 时间点出现，表示将来的事

   will sail 一般将来时



2. We**' ll meet** him **at the harbour** early in the morning.

   we will meet

   at the harbour 表示地点使用介词at

   early in the morning 一大早，清晨



3. He **will be** in his small boat, Topsail.

   Topsail 同位语，解释 boat.



4. It **has sailed** across the Atlantic **many times**.

   many times 许多次

   该句中 has sailed 时态是现在完成时，表示现在的之前有许多次的意思。



5. Captain Alison **will set out** at eight o' clock, so we**' ll have** plenty of time.

   set out = set off 开始，出发

   will have 一般将来时

   plenty of 不仅表示许多，而且言外之余还很充足。

   plenty of time / ideas



6. He **will be away** **for two months**.

   will + be 一般将来时

   be away 离开，**be动词+词组 通常表示状态，而状态是可以持续的。**

   for + 时间，表示持续了一段时间。

   注意：对比错误写法

   可以写成 He will leave，但是不能写成 ~~He will leave for two months~~. 

   因为 leave 是非延续性动词，不能接时间。

   在英语中有一类动词叫瞬间动词（也称为终止性动词、非延续性动词）。动作是瞬间结束而不是持续的动作。



7. We are very proud of him.

   be proud of ... 感到骄傲

   

8. He will **take part in** an important race across the Atlantic.

   take part in ... 参与，参加

   race 专用于比速度的比赛





## 知识拓展

### 一般将来时

- 一般将来时 be(am/is/are) going to + do 比 will 更强调 事先有预兆，事先有计划。

  例句：

  It is so dark outside. It **is going to** rain.

  I have got an offer. I **am going to** study abroad next year.



- 一般将来时 will + do 表示临时决定

  Be careful! The box is too heavy. I **will** help you.

  Will you marry me?



### 小练习

- 练习一

  A: What (you, do) `______` tomorrow?

  > 疑问句，将助动词提前。
  >
  > will you do / are you going to do

  B: I (work) `______` on my report.

  > will work / am going to work



- 练习二

  A: (you, finish) `______` this exercise soon?

  exercise n. 运动；练习

  > soon 通常表示不久之后的将来
  >
  > Will you finish / Are you going to finish

  B: Yes, I (finish) `______` it in less than a minute.

  > will finish / am going to finish



- 练习三

  A: Where (Alex, be) `______` later tonight?

  > will Alex be / is Alex going to be

  B: He (be) `______` at Kim's house.

  > will be / is going to be



### be动词短语

be动词带领的词组，通常强调持续的状态。



- 处于离开的状态

  He will **be away** for two months. 

  

- 处于回来的状态

  I' m going out now. I' ll **be back** at six o' clock. 

  I' ll **be out** all morning. 

  

- be in 可以表示在家的意思。

  I went to Ted' s house and asked to see him but he **was**n' t **in**.  



- be over 完结

  Why don' t you forget about it? It**' s** all **over**.



- be on 上映，在进行

  What' **s on** at the local cinema this week?



- be up to 胜任

  She is very ill. She can' t start work yet.

  She **is** not **up to** is.
