## 语法讲解

### 本课重点

1. 形容词副词的用法
2. 形容词副词的比较级和最高级



### 形容词/副词的作用

1. 形容词（adj.）修饰名词

   This is a **wonderful** world.

   We are living in a **beautiful new** house in the country.

   前两句形容词作定语，帮助名词确定范围。

   

   Their sleeping bags were **warm and comfortable**.

   单个be动词出现，因此该句为主系表结构，形容词作表语。



2. 副词修（adv.）饰动词/句子/其他的形容词或副词

   Roy acted **quickly**.

   **Suddenly**, one of the children kicked a ball.

   It rained **continually** and it was often **bitterly** cold.

   The students from elite universites catch up **very** **quickly**.



### 形容词副词的三种比较级别

原级比较、比较级、最高级



#### 形容词副词的最高级

#### 形式

```
adj. / adv. 的最高级 + （介词短语表示比较的范围）

（...范围内）最...

通常用于三者或以上的比较
```

#### 例句

1. My younger sister is **the tallest** one in her class.

   the tallest 最高的



2. **The most serious** problem of the project is the lack of power.

   serious 严重的

   该项目的最严重的问题是缺乏电力。



3. He is the tallest **of** all the students.

   He is the tallest **in** our class.

   of + 比较的对象之间

   in + 地点、场所



#### 练习介词搭配

Which is the longest river **in** the world?

This is the finest picture **of** them all.

This stereo is the most expensive **of** all the ones in the shop.

He is the best boxer **in** our town.



### 形容词副词的比较级

#### 形式

```
adj. / adv. 的比较级 + （than 比较的对象）

（和...相比）更...

通常用于两者之间的比较
```



#### 例句一

1. I am **taller** than you.
2. Air tickets are **more expensive** than train tickets.
3. Sometimes machines can perform **better** than human beings.

#### 例句二

过去和现在进行比较

1. I am fatter than before.

2. I am fatter than I was.

   主语+动作，用时态来区分，前后比较对象高度一致。am 表示现在，was表示过去。

3. I am fatter than I used to be.

   used to do sth. 后接动词原形，表示过去常常做某事

   be源自I was，变回原形be

   这里I used to be = I was，表示过去的状态

#### 例句三

过去和现在进行比较

1. The traffic block is more serious **than before**.

   The traffic block 交通拥堵

2. The traffic block is more serious **than it was**.

   it = the traffic block

3. The traffic block is more serious **than it used to be**.

#### 例句四

真实的状况和想象进行比较

1. You are smarter than they think. 

   你比他们想象的要更加聪明。

2. The project took longer than I expected.

   这个项目比我预期花费了更多时间。

#### 例句五

1. 北京的人口比天津多

   The population of Beijing is larger **than Tianjin**. （错误）

   The population of Beijing is larger **than that of Tianjin**.（正确）

   that of = population of

2. 去London的游客比去Bath的更多

   The visitors to London is more than Bath. （错误）

   The visitors to London is more than those to Bath.（正确）

   复数名词用those

   those to = visitors to




## 课文讲解

1. Joe Sanders has **the most beautiful** garden in our town.

   in our town 在我们镇上，表示范围。

   the most beautiful 最漂亮的，最高级。



2. Nearly everybody enters for '**The Nicest Garden Competition**' each year, but Joe wins every time.

   `enters for` enter表示进入，for表示目的，这里表示报名加入。



3. Bill Frith's garden is **larger than Joe's**.

   Bill Frith's garden is larger than Joe's garden

   比较的对象要对等，不能去掉 `'s`



4. Bill works **harder than Joe** and grows **more flowers and vegetables**, but Joe's garden is **more interesting**.

   在上下文中，只要比较的范围能够默认理解，可以不写。



5. Every year I enter for the garden competition too, and I always win a little prize for **the worst garden** in the town!

   the worst 最糟糕



## 知识拓展

不规则变化的比较级和最高级

|                    | 比较级  | 最高级   | 好，坏，多，少，老，远。                                     |
| ------------------ | ------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| good / well        | better  | best     | 好。                                                         |
| bad / ill          | worse   | worst    | 坏。                                                         |
| many / much        | more    | most     | 多。                                                         |
| littler / a little | less    | least    | 少。a little 表示有一点，比较积极的说法。littler 没剩下多少，消极否定说法，意思是更少了。 |
| old                | older   | oldest   | 老。表示人或物                                               |
| old                | elder   | eldest   | 表示人年长                                                   |
| far                | farther | farthest | 远。表示距离远                                               |
| far                | further | furthest | 除了距离远，还含有抽象的意思，譬如某事意义深远。可表达程度、数量等。 |
