# Lesson 25 Do the English speak English?

## 单词讲解

1. railway n. 铁路

   railway station 火车站



2. porter n. 搬运工



2. several quantifier 几个

   several 修饰限定名词的数量，表示几个，后加可数名词复数形式

   several times 几次

   several people 几个人



3. foreigner n. 外国人

   foreign adj. 外国的，外国人的

   a foreign language 

   TOEFL = Test Of English as a Foreign Language



4. wonder v. 感到奇怪

   feel surprised 感到奇怪

   want to know 想知道
