### 课文讲解

After I **had had** lunch at a village pub, I looked for my bag.

> had had，have的过去完成时，表示过去的之前吃过午饭。

I **had left** it on a chair **beside** the door and now it wasn' t there!

> had left，
>
> beside prep. 在...旁边
>
> beside the door 在门旁边
>
> **Besides** this book, I have some others.
>
> besides prep. 除...之外

As I was **looking for** it, the landlord came in.

> looking for 带着目的到处看，寻找的意思。

“Did you **have** a good meal?” he asked.

> have 是实义动词，把助动词提前变疑问，表示吃了一顿好饭吗？

"Yes, thank you," I answered, "but I can' t pay the bill. I **haven' t got** my bag."

The landlord smiled and immediately went out.

In a few minutes he returned **with my bag** and gave it back to me.

> with prep 和...一起；带有
>
> with my bag 带着包

"I' m very sorry," he said.

"My dog **had taken** it into the garden. He often does this!"

> taken it into the garden 带进花园
>
> had taken 过去完成时，表示过去的之前就带到花园里。





## 知识拓展

### 短语动词 give

He returned with my bag and **gave** it **back** to me.

> gave back
>
> give sth. back 表示把...给回来

**Give in** your exercise books to me.

> give in 上交

He can' t continue fighting. He will soon **give in**.

> give in 投降

I **gave away** my collection of stamps to the little boy.

> gave away 给出去，表示送给别人
>
> stamp n. 邮票；复数 stamps

I have **given up** smoking.

> given up 放弃，现在完成时，表示现在的之前已经放弃抽烟。即戒烟。

Three of our officers **gave** them **up** to the enemy.

> enemy n. 敌人
>
> 放弃自己，交给敌人，表示投降。

   > grew up 过去时态，表示过去已经长大。

   > composition n. 作文

4. We were losing the battle but we did not give (   ) .

1. back
2. away
